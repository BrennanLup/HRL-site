Providing Laurier students with the tools they need to succeed in HR.

Learn

Human Resources Laurier is centered around a 3 tier model: Learn. Develop. Grow. We strive to educate students on the exciting career opportunities in Human Resources, aid those who wish to break into the industry, and differentiate the Lazardis students at large through skill building opportunities.

Develop

Whether students are interested in HR or another field of business, we pride ourselves as being an organization that enables the unique skills they hold and provide support in developing their skill set so they flourish wherever their careers take them!

Grow

As students in the BBA program, we’ve recognized the gap present between Human Resources and the business world. We are determined to mend this through our events and interactions with business professionals and students.
