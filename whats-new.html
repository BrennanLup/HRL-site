HRPA Industry Night: Early November

This event will be a partnership with Laurier’s Career Center as an online event where a representative from HRPA will give a keynote presentation and HR professionals will be present for questions and networking opportunities. The presentation will be about the HRPA designation and steps students can take to achieve their certificate. Alumni from the club and Laurier in general who work in the field of HR will be invited to speak about what to expect when starting careers in HR right after graduation and more specifically about types of positions available for new-grads and how to land those roles.

Recruitment Panel: Mid Janurary

This event will focus on the hiring and selection processes in the financial services industry. Recruiters will be brought in from various financial institutions to discuss the hidden job market, decrypting a job description, and how to stand out as an applicant. The event will allow students to dive deeper into the recruitment process and become an attractive candidate in an extremely competitive industry. The night will close with networking where students and HR professionals will be able to discuss what they have learned and create meaningful connections.

Change Management Workshop: Early March

This event will focus on bringing HR into a current and applicable light given the quickly changing environment. HR professionals and consultants will be brought in to discuss how organizations cope with change within the organization, new policies that are implemented and adaptations made with new systems/technology and communication. With the impacts COVID-19 has had on our workplace, this event will cater to the effects the pandemic has had on the workforce and what “returning to work” will look like for the world of business. Students will get the opportunity to learn how HR works behind the scenes during major economic changes and the necessity of the field of people, change and culture.
